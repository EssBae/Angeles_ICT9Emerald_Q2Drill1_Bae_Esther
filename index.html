Emergency Go Bag Essentials
Emergency Water & Food
Water and food are vital for survival—pack at least 1 gallon of water per person per day and a 3-day supply of non-perishable food for energy and hydration.
First Aid Kit
It helps treat minor injuries and prevent infections—bring bandages, antiseptic wipes, pain relievers, gauze, adhesive tape, and any personal medicines for at least three days.
Hygiene Kit
It helps maintain cleanliness and prevent illness—bring toothbrush, toothpaste, soap, hand sanitizer, wet wipes, tissue, and feminine products for at least three days.
Extra Clothes
They keep you clean, dry, and comfortable—pack an extra set of clothes, underwear, socks, and a light jacket suitable for the weather.
